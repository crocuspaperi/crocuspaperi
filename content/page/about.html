My name is Irina, I am a watercolor and line art illustrator based in Finland. I'm Russian originally, and I came to Finland to do a Master's Degree. I ended up drawing flowers and designing beautiful stationery with my illustrations.

I create wedding stationery, postcards and greeting cards, logos, business cards and branding materials. And anything else you can put floral and botanical illustrations on.

I offer three types of services

  • ready - made designs
  • custom designs
  • poscards and greeting cards with my illustrations in my online shop and in shops around Finland

How I work

1. Idea

You describe your idea to me, we define the final product to be delivered and the schedule for our project

2. First options

I send you the first options. I make the necessary corrections according to your comments. I use Dropbox for uploading and commenting on designs.

3. Editing

We correct and edit the designs to arrive to the final product. Up to 3 rounds of corrections are usually enough.

4. Delivery

I upload your designs to a Dropbox folder, where they are available for 2 weeks for you to download. Prints are delivered to you by post (if you live outside Helsinki area) or you pick them up at Kukkakauppa Floranna at Runeberginkatu 30, Helsinki. Please check the work hours on the website.

5. Payment

Together with your prints you receive the invoice. If you order a digital product, you receive the invoice by email.
